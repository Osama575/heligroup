Who we are

The only stand-alone Part 145 and Part 147 organisation in the UK.

THEHELIGROUP started in 2017 as a consultancy and grew into something the UK didn't otherwise have: combined helicopter maintenance and type training, under one roof, independent of any operator, AOC, or corporate parent.

That independence is the whole point. No fleet of our own pulls engineers off your aircraft on a bad day. We mobilise quickly, stay competitive, and answer to you. The team are engineers, supervisors, mentors, instructors and assessors with decades on the airframe.
