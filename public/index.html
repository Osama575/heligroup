Who we are

Independent maintenance & training, under one roof.

THEHELIGROUP began in 2017 as a consultancy and has grown into the only stand-alone combined Part 145 and Part 147 organisation in the UK — independent of any operator, AOC, or large corporate commitment.

That independence is what lets us mobilise quickly, stay competitive, and deliver punctual, reliable engineering and training to helicopter operators worldwide. Our team are seasoned engineers, supervisors, mentors, instructors and assessors with decades of hands-on experience.
